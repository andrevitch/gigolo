ABOUT US

To make a long story short...

The Walper Hotel has stood the test of time. Although ownership has changed over the past 126 years, the ideals of its founder have endured—by meticulously combining luxury and history. The Walper remains among the finest and most celebrated boutique hotels in Canada.

But despite its illustrious reputation, it's not without its dark side. The presence of Gigolo is still said to be felt around the property. On countless occasions, guests have claimed to see his shadowy figure seated at the grand piano in the lounge, or using the hotel's main elevator. As such, our masterful barbers treat every patron with the same premium service and memorable experience, knowing that Abel is always watching. Don't believe us? Come take a seat.
